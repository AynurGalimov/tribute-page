Yuri Gagarin: First Man in Space

Here is a timeline of Mr. Gagarin's life:

  • 1934 - Born in Klushino(160 km west of Moscow)
  • 1942 - Klushino is attacked and occupied by the German army
  • 1943 - Yuri's brother Valentin and sister Zoya are abducted by the SS
  • Late 1943 - Valentin and Zoya escape the labour camp and are drafted as volunteers into the Soviet Army.
  • 1945 - The Gagarin family move to Gzhatsk
  • Late 1945 - Valentin and Zoya return home
  • 1949 - Gagarin begins his studies at the Lyubertsy Vocational School No.10
  • 1951 - Yuri enrolls at Saratov Industrial Technical School
  • 1954 - Gagarin signed up at the Saratov Oblast Flying Club
  • 1955 - Yuri performs his first parachute jump
  • 1955 - Yuri's first Solo Flight in YAK 18
  • 1955 - Gagarin was drafted into the Soviet Army and sent to study in Orenburg at the Voroshilov 1st Chkalovsk Military Aviation School
  • 1956 - Gagarin makes rank of Aviation Cadet Sergeant and is permitted to fly
  • 1959 - Gagarin Submits request for cosmonaut training
  • 1960 - Gagarin is approved for cosmonaut training
  • 1961 - Yuri Alexeyevich Gagarin orbits the Earth in VOSTOK-1 capsule
  • 1961 - Gagarin is awarded the first British Interplanetary Society gold medal
  • 1968 - Colonel Yuri Gagarin's MiG15UTI crashed, killing Gagarin and his instructor, Colonel Vladimir Seryogin
